What Your AI Can See
Your AI assistant can read the files in your project — like documents, spreadsheets, and code. The more relevant files it can see, the better answers it gives. This is called context.
Think of it like briefing a new team member: the more background you share, the more useful their work will be. You're in charge of what your AI knows.
Context Engineering in 30 seconds
1. Put relevant docs in your project folder
2. Write a short instructions file describing your goals
3. Your AI reads these automatically — no extra steps
That's it. You're now doing context engineering. 🎉

Save Your Work
Git saves snapshots of your project called commits. Think of them as "save points" in a video game — you can always go back. Instead of memorizing commands, just tell Roo what you want.
Try these:
"Save my current work with a note about what I changed"
Send to Roo →
"Show me what I've changed since my last save"
Send to Roo →
💡 Pro tip
Save often. Roo will write clear descriptions of each save automatically. If anything goes wrong, you can always say "undo my last change" or "go back to how things were an hour ago."
