## Connect Your First Tool

PM Code connects to the tools you already use so your AI assistant can access real project data.

### Supported connectors

- **Jira** — issues, sprints, backlogs
- **GitHub** — repos, PRs, issues
- **Monday.com** — boards, items, updates
- **Aha!** — features, releases, ideas
- **Tavily** — web search and research

### How to connect

1. Open the **Connectors** panel from the sidebar
2. Pick a tool and enter your API credentials
3. Click **Test Connection** to verify it works

Your credentials are stored locally in `~/.pmcode/.env` and never leave your machine.
