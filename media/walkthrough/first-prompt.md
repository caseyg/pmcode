## Try Talking to Your AI

Now that you're set up, try sending a prompt to see what your AI assistant can do.

### Example prompts to try

- "What tasks are assigned to me this sprint?"
- "Summarize the most recent PRs in our repo"
- "Triage the top 10 ideas in my backlog"
- "Give me a standup summary from yesterday"

### Tips

- Be specific about what you want — the AI works best with clear requests
- You can reference your connected tools by name (e.g., "check Jira for...")
- Use **Skills** from the sidebar for pre-built workflows like PRD writing or sprint retros
