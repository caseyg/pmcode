## Meet Your AI Assistant

PM Code works alongside **Roo Code**, an AI assistant that lives right inside VS Code. Think of Roo as a knowledgeable teammate who understands your product work.

### What Roo can do for you

| Task | Example |
|------|---------|
| Answer questions about your project | "What's the status of the auth feature?" |
| Pull data from connected tools | "Show me open Jira tickets for this sprint" |
| Run PM workflows | "Triage the top 10 ideas in our backlog" |
| Draft documents | "Write a PRD for the onboarding redesign" |
| Research competitors | "Compare our pricing to the top 3 competitors" |

### How it works

1. You type a request in natural language
2. Roo reads your project files and connected tools
3. Roo responds with structured output you can use immediately

### Get started

Click **Open Roo Code** above to open the chat sidebar, then say hello. Try something like:

> "Hi! I'm a product manager. What can you help me with?"

No coding knowledge required. Just describe what you need in plain English.
