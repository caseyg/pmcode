## Meet Your AI Assistant

PM Code works alongside **Roo Code**, an AI assistant that lives right inside VS Code.

Think of Roo as a knowledgeable teammate who can:

- Read your project files and documentation
- Talk to your connected tools (Jira, GitHub, Monday, etc.)
- Help you get work done faster with natural language

### Get started

Click the **Roo Code** icon in the Activity Bar on the left side of VS Code to open the chat interface. This is where you'll have conversations with your AI assistant throughout your workday.
